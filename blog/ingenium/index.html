Overview



 
Ingenium is a platform for engineers who want to work as freelancers in all fields.


It has 3 aspects:

• Clients can search for engineers and hire them.

• Engineers can search for jobs and apply.

• Interns have the chance to be hired by expert engineers who can create a team for each task.



 

The Challenge



 
Find job Safety First Organization
It’s not easy to find a suitable job or Freelancer with a lot of fields and options, it was important to make the search process easier. Each client and freelancer should grantee the payment process and the working process from the start of the project. The task process from the start needed to be organized as much as possible to make the task easy and smooth for clients, freelancers, and interns.

Design Solutions



 
Detailed search Filters


To make sure that the search for a job or a freelancer is detailed

to make finding a job matching the skills easier.




 


 

 
Job Details and contract


Before starting the job the client has to send a detailed job contract

to the freelancer and the freelancer can see job details and decide to accept the offer or not.




 


 

 
Work Diary


The Work Diary is the place where all the tasks are organized and the client

can track the time to see work progress.
